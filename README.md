# MacroSignal

**The honest macro copilot that reads the world before the crowd does.**

Most market tools sell confidence: blinking buy signals, backtests too beautiful
to be true, an AI that "predicts" prices. MacroSignal is built on the opposite
principle — *an LLM never produces a number the machine acts on.* Every score,
state, and forecast comes from frozen, hand-checkable formulas over free primary
data, replayed across 15 years with strict as-of discipline so it can fail its
own tests. And it does: of the engine ideas tried, most were killed on evidence,
not opinion.

It scans ~20 free data streams across nine markets — futures and equity themes —
and shadows the people who move first: large speculators (CFTC), the patient
whales (13F, with each fund's single highest-conviction "Best Idea"), corporate
insiders (opportunistic-vs-routine, CFO-weighted, size-and-fraction-gated sells),
and foreign governments (weekly Fed custody, Treasury-auction demand). Each
Saturday it runs itself and writes one plain-language page: **"The world right
now"** — every side read together, with the disagreements called out — plus
honest forward base rates and a Monte Carlo simulation of what history did next.
No jargon, no urgency tricks, ~11 considered signals a year.

Its default answer is usually *"nothing changed — do nothing,"* and that
restraint is the product. MacroSignal isn't here to make you trade more. It's
here to tell you, honestly, when the world is actually shifting — and to protect
you from the noise the rest of the time.
